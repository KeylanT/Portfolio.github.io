NBA Award Predictions

Leveraging linear regression, Selenium, and Pandas to forecast NBA MVP, DPOY, and ROTY, unraveling statistical patterns for season accolades in basketball.

December 23, 2023 Keylan Taylor

The designed solution is a sophisticated tool for analyzing and predicting data in professional basketball. It cleverly uses various Python libraries to handle data, perform statistical analysis, and apply machine learning techniques. The main goal is to uncover valuable insights from basketball stats, covering advanced player metrics, individual game performance, and team-based analytics. To start, the tool gathers and organizes advanced player statistics and per-game metrics from basketball-reference.com. The collected data undergoes careful processing, including normalization to ensure fair comparisons among different players and teams. Team-based statistics are also obtained, offering a comprehensive view of overall team performance. The results are neatly stored in CSV files, making it easy to manage and share the data. Machine learning models play a crucial role in the solution. Pre-trained models are implemented to predict the likelihood of players winning prestigious awards like Most Valuable Player (MVP), Defensive Player of the Year (DPOY), and Rookie of the Year (ROTY). These predictions provide valuable insights into potential award recipients. Moreover, the solution identifies rookie players and assesses their chances of winning awards, offering a thorough perspective on emerging talent in the basketball scene. The final outcomes are exported into CSV files and HTML tables, which can be viewed below. These HTML tables, enriched with dynamic content, can seamlessly integrate into a web-based portfolio or application. The model-generated predictions include top contenders for MVP, DPOY, and ROTY, each accompanied by associated probabilities. In summary, this solution combines web scraping, statistical analysis, and machine learning to distill actionable insights from the extensive realm of basketball statistics. It ultimately enhances our understanding of player performance and award predictions.
